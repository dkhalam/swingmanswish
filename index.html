About

Swingmanswish is the brainchild of a serious dedication to the NBA. Built by fans, for fans. We seek to provide a hub for all basketball enthusiasts by delivering accessible statistics, our pick of freshly brewed league news, stunning highlights, and last but certainly not least, a prime time fantasy experience. Unsatisfied with other fantasy basketball services, we aim to make our game as simple as possible. Each season, you can pick your 'starting five'. Players are ranked by skill as a 5, 4, 3, 2, 1. This is based on our rank of the top 15 projected scorers at the beginning of the season. But, if you choose to select a player outside of this list, their rank automatically counts as a 1. Simply have your players ranks add up to 15, and you've got your team! Scoring is the objective here, no fussing around with rebounds, assists, etc. It's your team versus the next guys. Points are added up throughout the NBA schedule and if your team has more points than the next guy, you win. There will be more to come, this is just the baseline for this super addictive app.
